## Strength

### 1. DB Bench Press
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 15 | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 12 | Moderate | 6 |
| 3 | 10 | Moderate-Heavy | 7 |
| 4 | 8 | Heavy | 8 |

**Rest:** 90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** SL Trunk Rotation between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** DB Bench 4 x 12 (lighter weight)
> - **Intermediate:** DB Bench as written
> - **Advanced:** Add pause at bottom or increase load

### 2. BB RDL to Wide Grip Bent Row
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 8-12 | Moderate | 6 |
| 2 | 8-12 | Moderate | 7 |
| 3 | 8-12 | Moderate | 7 |

**Rest:** 75 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Kneeling Hip Flexor/Hamstring stretch between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Separate RDL and Rows (3 x 10 each)
> - **Intermediate:** Combo as written
> - **Advanced:** Increase load or add pause at bottom of RDL
