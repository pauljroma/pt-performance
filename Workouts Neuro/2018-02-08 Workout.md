### 1. Single Leg Squat
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10e | Bodyweight/Light | 6 |
| 2 | 10e | Bodyweight/Light | 7 |
| 3 | 10e | Light | 7 |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Quad Pulls between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Assisted SL Squat (TRX or box) 3 x 8e
> - **Intermediate:** SL Squat to box as written
> - **Advanced:** Full pistol squat or weighted

> [!info] Demo
> Sit back to box/bench, single leg. Control the descent, drive through heel.
