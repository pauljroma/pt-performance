# Strength Blocks

Select 1-2 strength blocks per workout. Match the rep scheme to your current phase.

## Rep Scheme Guide

| Phase | Sets x Reps | Rest | Notes |
|-------|-------------|------|-------|
| Foundation | 3-4 x 10-12 | 60-90s | Focus on form |
| Strength | 4-5 x 5-8 | 2-3 min | Progressive overload |
| Power | 3-5 x 3-5 | 2-3 min | Explosive, submaximal |
| Hypertrophy | 3-4 x 8-15 | 60-90s | Moderate load, high volume |

---

# Lower Body Blocks

## Block S1: Squat Dominant
**Primary:** Back Squat, Front Squat, or Goblet Squat
**Accessory:** Quad Pulls between sets

*Foundation:* 4 x 10
*Strength:* 5 x 5 (building: 10, 8, 6, 4, 4)
*Power:* 5 x 3 @ 70-80%

---

## Block S2: Split Stance
**Primary:** Split Squats or Walking Lunges
**Accessory:** Quad Pulls between sets

*Foundation:* 3 x 10e
*Strength:* 4 x 8e (add load progressively)
*Power:* 4 x 6e (explosive up)

---

## Block S3: Hinge Dominant
**Primary:** Deadlift (conventional, sumo, or trap bar)
**Accessory:** Hamstring Walks or T.T.P.

*Foundation:* 4 x 8-10
*Strength:* Building - 10, 10, 8, 8, 6
*Power:* 5 x 3 @ 70-80% (speed focus)

---

## Block S4: Single Leg Hinge
**Primary:** Single Leg Deadlift or Single Leg RDL
**Accessory:** SL Bridge x 10e between sets

*Foundation:* 3 x 8e (bodyweight or light)
*Strength:* 4 x 8e (DB or KB)

---

## Block S5: Slant Bar / Quad Extension
**Primary:** Slant Bar Triple Extension or Leg Extension
**Accessory:** Quad Pulls

*All Phases:* 3-4 x 10
*Focus:* Terminal knee extension, VMO activation

---

# Upper Body Push Blocks

## Block S6: Horizontal Push
**Primary:** Bench Press (BB, DB, or KB)
**Accessory:** PVC Passovers or Snow Angels

*Foundation:* 4 x 10
*Strength:* Building - 12, 10, 8, 6, 4
*Power:* 4 x 6 (explosive)

---

## Block S7: Vertical Push
**Primary:** Overhead Press (strict) or Push Press
**Accessory:** Lat Stretch between sets

*Foundation:* 4 x 10
*Strength:* 4 x 8 or Building - 10, 8, 6, 6
*Power:* 5 x 5 Push Press

---

## Block S8: Single Arm Press
**Primary:** 1/2 Kneel SA Press or SA DB Bench
**Accessory:** PVC Passovers

*All Phases:* 4 x 8-10e
*Focus:* Core anti-rotation, unilateral balance

---

# Upper Body Pull Blocks

## Block S9: Horizontal Pull
**Primary:** Bent Over Row (BB or DB) or Renegade Row
**Accessory:** Lat Stretch

*Foundation:* 4 x 10
*Strength:* 4 x 8 or Building - 10, 10, 8, 6
*Hypertrophy:* 4 x 12

---

## Block S10: Vertical Pull
**Primary:** Pull Ups (or TRX Rows if scaling)
**Accessory:** Banded Lat Stretch x :20 each

*Foundation:* 4 x AMRAP or 4 x 8 TRX
*Strength:* 4 x 6-8 (add weight if needed)
*Building:* 6, 6, 6, 4

---

## Block S11: Single Arm Row
**Primary:** SA KB/DB Row or SA Cable Row
**Accessory:** Lat Stretch

*All Phases:* 4 x 8-10e

---

# Full Body / Power Blocks

## Block S12: Turkish Get Up
**Primary:** TGU
**Accessory:** SL Rotation between sets

*Foundation:* Bodyweight practice - 3e x 3
*Strength:* 3e x 3 (moderate weight)
*Heavy:* 3e x 2-3 (challenging weight)

---

## Block S13: KB Complex
**Primary:** KB Swing → Goblet Squat → Push Press
**Accessory:** Quick transitions

*Conditioning Focus:* 5 of each x 4 rounds
*Strength Focus:* 8 of each x 3 rounds (heavier)

---

## Block S14: Barbell Complex
**Primary:** DL → Row → Clean → Front Squat → Press
**Accessory:** 5 reps each, no rest between exercises

*Light/Technique:* Empty bar, 3-4 rounds
*Moderate:* 65-95 lbs, 3 rounds

---

## Block S15: Power Development
**Primary:** Box Jumps, Med Ball Slams, or KB Swings
**Accessory:** Full recovery between sets

*Power:* 5 x 5 Box Jumps (step down)
*Power:* 4 x 10 MB Slams
*Power/Conditioning:* 5 x 15 KB Swings
