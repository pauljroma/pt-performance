## Strength

### 1. Deadlift (Building)
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10 | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 10 | Moderate | 6 |
| 3 | 8 | Moderate+ | 7 |
| 4 | 8 | Working | 7-8 |
| 5 | 6 | Heavy | 8 |

**Rest:** 2-3 min between sets
**Accessory:** Hamstring Walk between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** KB Deadlift 5 x 8
> - **Intermediate:** BB Deadlift as written
> - **Advanced:** Add deficit or pause at knee

> [!info] Demo
> Neutral spine, brace core, push floor away. Bar stays close to body. Lockout with glutes, don't hyperextend.

### 2. Renegade Rows
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 8e | Light | 6 |
| 2 | 8e | Moderate | 7 |
| 3 | 8e | Moderate | 7 |
| 4 | 8e | Moderate+ | 8 |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Lat Stretch between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** SA DB Row 4 x 10e (supported)
> - **Intermediate:** Renegade Rows as written
> - **Advanced:** Add push-up between rows, heavier DBs

> [!info] Demo
> Plank position, wide feet for stability. Row one DB while pressing other into floor. Minimize hip rotation.
