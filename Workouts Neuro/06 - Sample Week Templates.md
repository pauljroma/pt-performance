## Deload Week Template

### All Days (3x per week)
| Block | Selection | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Warmup | W8 | Mobility-first |
| Dynamic | Any | Full range |
| Strength | 50% of normal volume | Light loads |
| Conditioning | C16 or Light cardio | 20 min easy |
| Finisher | F7 + F8 | Extended mobility |

**Deload Guidelines:**
- Reduce loads by 40-50%
- Reduce volume by 40-50%
- Focus on movement quality
- Extra mobility and recovery work
- Active recovery activities (walking, swimming, yoga)
