## Strength

### 1. Single Leg Deadlifts
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 8e | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 8e | Moderate | 6 |
| 3 | 8e | Moderate | 7 |
| 4 | 8e | Moderate+ | 7 |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** TTP (Toe Touch Progression) between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Kickstand RDL (back toe on ground) 4 x 8e
> - **Intermediate:** SL Deadlift as written
> - **Advanced:** SL Deadlift with contralateral load, add pause at bottom

> [!info] Demo
> Balance on one leg, hinge at hip, reach back leg straight behind. Keep hips square.

| 3 | 8e | Moderate | 7 |

> [!tip] Scaling Options

> [!info] Demo
> Support on bench, pull to hip, squeeze lat at top. Control the negative.
