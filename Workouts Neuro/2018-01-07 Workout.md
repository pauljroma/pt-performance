## Strength

### 1. Walking Lunges
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10e | Bodyweight | 5 |
| 2 | 10e | Light DBs | 6 |
| 3 | 8e | Moderate DBs | 7 |
| 4 | 6e | Heavy DBs | 8 |

**Rest:** 90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Quad Pulls between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Stationary lunges 4 x 8e bodyweight
> - **Intermediate:** Walking lunges as written
> - **Advanced:** Barbell walking lunges or add deficit

> [!info] Demo
> Step through, back knee nearly touches floor. Keep torso upright, drive through front heel.

### 2. Bent Over Row (BOR)
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10 | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 10 | Moderate | 6 |
| 3 | 8 | Moderate+ | 7 |
| 4 | 6 | Heavy | 8 |

**Rest:** 90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Lat Stretch between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** DB Rows 4 x 10e supported
> - **Intermediate:** BB BOR as written
> - **Advanced:** Pendlay rows (from floor each rep)

> [!info] Demo
> Hinge at hips ~45°, pull to lower chest/upper abs, squeeze shoulder blades.
