# Warmup Blocks

Select one warmup block based on your primary movement for the day.

---

## Block W1: General Cardio Warmup
**Duration:** 5-7 min | **Best for:** Any day

- Row/Bike 2-3 min (easy pace)
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 20 Jumping Jacks

---

## Block W2: Lower Body Focus
**Duration:** 7-10 min | **Best for:** Squat/Deadlift days

- Row 500m or Bike 1000m
- Monster Walks x 20 steps
- 20e SL Glute Bridge
- 15 Air Squats with pause

---

## Block W3: Upper Body Focus
**Duration:** 7-10 min | **Best for:** Press/Pull days

- Jump Rope 2 min
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 TRX Rows
- Arm Circles 10 forward/10 back
- Band Pull Aparts x 15

---

## Block W4: Power/Athletic
**Duration:** 8-10 min | **Best for:** Explosive/plyometric days

- Run 2 laps or 400m
- High Knees 20 yards
- Butt Kicks 20 yards
- A-Skip 20 yards
- Power Skip 20 yards

---

## Block W5: Movement Prep (Barbell)
**Duration:** 5 min | **Best for:** Heavy lifting days

Empty Bar Complex:
- RDL x 8
- Bent Row x 8
- Front Squat x 8
- Strict Press x 8
- Back Squat x 8

---

## Block W6: Extended Warmup
**Duration:** 10-12 min | **Best for:** Cold days or stiff mornings

**2 Rounds:**
- Row 300m
- Rear Lunge w/ Reach x 10
- Lateral Squat x 10
- 10 Push Ups
- 30 sec Plank

---

## Block W7: Core-Activated Warmup
**Duration:** 7 min | **Best for:** Days focusing on bracing

- Bike 2 min easy
- Dead Bug x 20 total
- Bird Dog x 10e
- Plank 30 sec
- Side Plank 20 sec each

---

## Block W8: Mobility-First Warmup
**Duration:** 8-10 min | **Best for:** Stiff/tight days

- Foam Roll (quads, lats, t-spine) 3 min
- Cat/Cow x 10
- World's Greatest Stretch x 5e
- Deep Squat Hold 30 sec
- Arm Circles and Shoulder CARs
