## Strength

### 1. DB Bench Press
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10 | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 10 | Moderate | 6 |
| 3 | 10 | Moderate | 7 |
| 4 | 10 | Moderate+ | 7 |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** PVC Passovers between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Push Ups 4 x 10 (knees ok)
> - **Intermediate:** DB Bench as written
> - **Advanced:** Add pause at bottom, or incline DB bench

> [!info] Demo
> Feet flat, slight arch in lower back. Lower to chest level, press straight up. Control the negative.

### 2. KB Front Squat
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10 | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 10 | Moderate | 6 |
| 3 | 10 | Moderate | 7 |
| 4 | 10 | Moderate+ | 7 |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Quad Pull between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Goblet Squat 4 x 10
> - **Intermediate:** Double KB Front Squat as written
> - **Advanced:** Add 2-sec pause at bottom

> [!info] Demo
> KBs in rack position, elbows up. Squat to depth, drive through heels. Keep core tight.
