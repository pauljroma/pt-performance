|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 12 | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 12 | Light | 5 |
| 3 | 12 | Light | 5 |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Quad Pull between sets
**Note:** *These should be Light - focus on form and depth*

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Air Squat 3 x 15 with pause at bottom
> - **Intermediate:** Goblet Squat as written (light)
> - **Advanced:** Heavier Goblet Squat or Front Squat 3 x 10

## Conditioning

### 100 Thrusters Total
| Exercise | Reps | Format |
|----------|------|--------|
| Thrusters | 100 total | Every minute do 3 burpees |

**Format:** Complete 100 thrusters, stopping every minute to perform 3 burpees
**Target Time:** 15-20 min

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** 50 thrusters, 2 burpees per minute, light weight
> - **Intermediate:** As written
> - **Advanced:** 100 thrusters, 5 burpees per minute, heavier weight
