# Dynamic Mobility Blocks

Select based on the movements in your strength block.

---

## Block D1: Lower Body / Squat Prep
**Duration:** 8-10 min

- Quad Pull + Hinge x 8e
- Hamstring Walks x 10
- Piriformis Stretch (walking) x 8e
- Side Lunges x 8e
- Deep Squat Hold w/ rotation 30 sec
- 30 Glute Bridges

---

## Block D2: Lower Body / Hinge Prep
**Duration:** 8-10 min

- Hamstring Walks x 10
- Single Leg RDL (no weight) x 8e
- Toy Soldiers x 10e
- PVC Goodmornings x 10
- Leg Swings (front/back) x 10e
- Leg Swings (side/side) x 10e

---

## Block D3: Upper Body / Push Prep
**Duration:** 8-10 min

- PVC Passovers x 15
- Arm Circles (forward/back) x 10 each
- Wall Slides x 10
- Push Up + Rotation x 8 total
- Inchworms x 8
- Spiderman + Reach x 8e

---

## Block D4: Upper Body / Pull Prep
**Duration:** 8-10 min

- PVC Figure 8s x 10
- Band Pull Aparts x 15
- Lat Stretch (hang or doorway) 30 sec
- Cat/Cow x 10
- Quadruped Trunk Rotation x 10e
- High Knee Pull to Lunge x 8e

---

## Block D5: Full Body Athletic
**Duration:** 10 min

- High Knee Pull x 10e
- Quad Pull x 10e
- Over/Under Fence x 8e
- Spiderman + Rotate x 8e
- A-Skip x 20 yards
- Carioca x 20 yards each direction
- High Knee March x 20 yards

---

## Block D6: Agility Focus
**Duration:** 8-10 min

- Side Slide w/ Arm Swing x 20 yards
- Lateral Plank Walk x 10e
- Lunge w/ Palm to Instep x 8e
- Carioca x 20 yards each
- A-Skip / Lateral A-Skip
- Backpedal x 20 yards
- High Bear Crawl x 10 yards

---

## Block D7: Rotation/Anti-Rotation Prep
**Duration:** 8 min

- Quadruped Trunk Rotation x 10e
- Supine Trunk Rotation x 10e
- Lunge + Twist x 8e
- PVC Figure 8s x 10
- Pallof Press Hold 20 sec each
- Plank w/ Shoulder Taps x 20

---

## Block D8: Hip Mobility Focus
**Duration:** 10 min

- 90/90 Hip Switches x 10
- Pigeon Stretch 30 sec each
- Frog Stretch 30 sec
- Hip Circles (quadruped) x 10e
- Hip Flexor Stretch (1/2 kneel) 30 sec each
- Deep Squat Prying 30 sec
- Cossack Squat x 8e
