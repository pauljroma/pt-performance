## Strength

### 1. Overhead Squat
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10 | PVC/Empty bar | 5 |
| 2 | 10 | Light | 6 |
| 3 | 10 | Light+ | 6 |
| 4 | 10 | Moderate | 7 |

**Rest:** 90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Pigeon stretch between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** PVC OH Squat 4 x 10 (focus on mobility)
> - **Intermediate:** BB OH Squat as written
> - **Advanced:** Add pause at bottom, or snatch grip

> [!info] Demo
> Wide grip, bar overhead and slightly behind. Squat to depth maintaining bar position. Requires shoulder/thoracic mobility.
