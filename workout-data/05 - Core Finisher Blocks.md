# Core & Finisher Blocks

Select one core block and/or one finisher to close your workout.

---

# Core Blocks (5-10 min)

## Block CF1: Anti-Extension Focus
**3 Rounds:**
- Dead Bug x 12e
- Plank Hold 30-45 sec
- Ab Wheel Rollout x 10 (or Fallouts)

---

## Block CF2: Anti-Rotation Focus
**3 Rounds:**
- Pallof Press x 10e
- Bird Dog x 10e
- Plank w/ Shoulder Taps x 20

---

## Block CF3: Rotation Focus
**3 Rounds:**
- Russian Twist x 15e
- MB Chops (1/2 kneel) x 8e
- Bar Rotations x 10

---

## Block CF4: Flexion Focus
**3 Rounds:**
- V Ups x 15
- Sit Ups x 20
- Hanging Knee Raise x 12

---

## Block CF5: Stability Focus
**3 Rounds:**
- Starfish x 10e
- Side Plank 30 sec each
- Single Leg Glute Bridge x 10e

---

## Block CF6: Full Core Circuit
**2-3 Rounds:**
- Dead Bug x 10e
- V Ups x 12
- Russian Twist x 12e
- Plank 30 sec
- Side Plank 20 sec each

---

## Block CF7: Quick Core (5 min)
- Plank 45 sec
- Dead Bug x 20 total
- Side Plank 30 sec each
- V Ups x 15

---

## Block CF8: TGU Core Finisher
- TGU x 3e (light weight, controlled)
- SL Rotation x 5e between sides

---

# Finisher Blocks (5-10 min)

## Block F1: Row Cashout
- 500m Row (moderate pace)
- Foam Roll 3-5 min

---

## Block F2: Sprint Cashout
- 20 cal Sprint Bike
- 1 min rest
- 20 cal Sprint Row
- Foam Roll

---

## Block F3: Rope & Mobility
- 2 Rounds:
  - Rope Climb x 1
  - 8 Burpees
  - 50 Jump Rope
- Foam Roll

---

## Block F4: Wall Sit Challenge
- 1 min Wall Sit
- 30 Med Ball Toe Taps
- Jump Rope x 150
- Foam Roll

---

## Block F5: Farmer's Carry Finisher
- 3-4 Laps Farmer's Carry (heavy)
- 20 KB Swings
- Foam Roll

---

## Block F6: Sled/Prowler Finisher
- 4 x Down and Back (moderate weight)
- Rest as needed between
- Light stretch

---

## Block F7: Mobility Finisher
- Foam Roll (quads, lats, t-spine) 3 min
- Pigeon Stretch 1 min each
- Hip Flexor Stretch 1 min each
- Child's Pose 1 min

---

## Block F8: Active Recovery Finisher
- Easy Row/Bike 5 min
- Full body stretch 5 min
- Focus on breathing

---

# Quick Reference: Selecting Core Work

| Goal | Recommended Blocks |
|------|-------------------|
| Low back issues | CF1, CF2 (anti-extension/rotation) |
| Athletic performance | CF2, CF5 (stability) |
| Six-pack aesthetics | CF3, CF4 (rotation/flexion) |
| Functional strength | CF6, CF8 (full circuit, TGU) |
| Time-crunched | CF7 (quick core) |

---

# Combining Blocks

**Example Full Workout Assembly:**

1. Warmup: W2 (Lower Body Focus)
2. Dynamic: D1 (Squat Prep)
3. Strength: S1 (Squat Dominant) + S4 (Single Leg Hinge)
4. Conditioning: C2 (Lower Body AMRAP)
5. Core: CF1 (Anti-Extension)
6. Finisher: F1 (Row Cashout)

**Total Time:** ~60 minutes
