## Strength

### 1. Negative Pull Ups
| Set | Reps | Tempo | RPE |
|-----|------|-------|-----|
| 1 | AMAP | 5 sec down | 7 |
| 2 | AMAP | 5 sec down | 7 |
| 3 | AMAP | 5 sec down | 8 |
| 4 | AMAP | 5 sec down | 8 |

**Rest:** 90-120 sec between sets
**Accessory:** PVC Passovers between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Band-assisted negatives or TRX Rows 4 x 10
> - **Intermediate:** Negative pull ups as written (5 sec)
> - **Advanced:** Weighted negatives or 8-10 sec count

> [!info] Demo
> Jump/step to top position, lower as slowly as possible (5 sec minimum). Focus on lat engagement.
