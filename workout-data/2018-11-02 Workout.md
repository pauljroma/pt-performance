| 2 | 8e | Moderate | 7 |

| 4 | 8e | Moderate | 7 |

**Accessory:** PVC Passover between sets

|----------|------|-------|
| Jump Rope | 50 | Single-unders |

| Lateral Skater | 15e | Explosive |
