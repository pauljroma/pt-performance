## Strength

### 1. Lunge (3-way)
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 12 total | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 12 total | Moderate | 6 |
| 3 | 12 total | Moderate | 7 |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Quad Pull between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Forward lunge only 3 x 8e
> - **Intermediate:** 3-way lunge as written (forward, lateral, reverse)
> - **Advanced:** Add weight or deficit

> [!info] Demo
> 3-way = forward lunge, lateral lunge, reverse lunge. Same leg, then switch.
