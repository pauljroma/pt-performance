## Conditioning - Pyramid AMRAP (20-25 min)

Complete 1 rep of each, then 2 reps of each, then 3 reps... continue building.

| Exercise | Pattern | Scaling (Beginner) | Scaling (Advanced) |
|----------|---------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Pull Up | 1, 2, 3... | Band assisted | Weighted |
| Burpees | 1, 2, 3... | Step out burpees | Add jump |
| MB Slams | 1, 2, 3... | Lighter ball | Heavier ball |
| Rev. Lunge + Press | 1, 2, 3... | No weight | Heavier KB |
| Sit Ups | 1, 2, 3... | Regular | V Ups |
| Push Ups | 1, 2, 3... | Knee push ups | Diamond push ups |
| Squat Jumps | 1, 2, 3... | Air squats | Weighted |

**Rest:** Minimal - move continuously
