## Core - 12 Days Style
| Day | Exercise | Reps | Notes |
|-----|----------|------|-------|
| 1 | TGU | 1e | Full movement |
| 2 | Box Jump/Step Up | 2 | Power |
| 3 | Russian Twist | 3e | Core rotation |
| 4 | TRX Row | 4 | Upper back |
| 5 | Push Up | 5 | Upper body |
| 6 | Back Lunge | 6e | Lower body |
| 7 | Mtn Climber | 7e | Core/cardio |
| 8 | Goblet Squat | 8 | Lower body |
| 9 | Sit Ups | 9 | Core flexion |
| 10 | KB Swing | 10 | Hip hinge |
| 11 | Thrusters | 11 | Full body |
| 12 | Burpees | 12 | Full body finisher |

**Format:** Like "12 Days of Christmas" - start at 1, add next exercise each round
- Round 1: 1 TGU each side
- Round 2: 2 Box Jumps + 1 TGU each side
- Round 3: 3 Russian Twists each side + 2 Box Jumps + 1 TGU each side
- Continue pattern through all 12 exercises

**Target Time:** 30-45 min

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Reduce movements to 8 or 10 days, half reps
> - **Intermediate:** As written
> - **Advanced:** Add load to all movements or increase rep scheme
