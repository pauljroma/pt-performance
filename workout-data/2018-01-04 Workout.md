## Strength

### 1. Good Mornings
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10 | Light (bar only) | 5 |
| 2 | 10 | Light+ | 6 |
| 3 | 10 | Moderate | 7 |
| 4 | 10 | Moderate | 7 |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** HS Walk between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** PVC Good Mornings 4 x 12, focus on hip hinge pattern
> - **Intermediate:** BB Good Mornings as written
> - **Advanced:** Add 2-sec pause at bottom, or tempo 3-1-2

> [!info] Demo
> Soft knees, push hips back, feel stretch in hamstrings. Bar on upper back (not neck). Keep chest up.

### 2. ½ Kneel SA Press
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10e | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 10e | Moderate | 6 |
| 3 | 10e | Moderate | 7 |

**Rest:** 60 sec between sets (switch sides immediately)
**Accessory:** PVC Passover between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Standing DB Press 3 x 10
> - **Intermediate:** ½ Kneel SA Press as written
> - **Advanced:** Add 2-sec hold at top, increase weight

> [!info] Demo
> Back knee down, squeeze glute on kneeling side. Press straight overhead, no leaning.
