## Build Your Workout

Every workout follows this structure. Pick one block from each category:

### 1. Warmup (5-10 min)
**[[01 - Warmup Blocks]]**
- Cardio activation
- Movement prep

### 2. Dynamic Mobility (10 min)
**[[02 - Dynamic Mobility Blocks]]**
- Movement-specific stretches
- Activation drills

### 3. Strength (20-25 min)
**[[03 - Strength Blocks]]**
- Primary compound lift
- Accessory work
- Rest/mobility between sets

### 4. Conditioning (15-20 min)
**[[04 - Conditioning Blocks]]**
- AMRAP, EMOM, Rounds, Intervals
- Match intensity to your phase

### 5. Core & Finisher (5-10 min)
**[[05 - Core Finisher Blocks]]**
- Core work
- Foam roll / stretch

## Programming Phases

| Phase | Focus | When to Use |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **Foundation** | Movement quality, base | Starting out or returning |
| **Strength** | Max strength, power | Ready to push heavy |
| **Power/Speed** | Explosive, conditioning | Peak performance |
| **Hypertrophy** | Muscle building | Volume accumulation |
| **Recovery** | Light work, mobility | Deload or transition |

## Quick Workout Ideas

### "I Have 30 Minutes"
1. Skip warmup cardio, go straight to dynamic
2. Pick ONE strength block (superset format)
3. 8-10 min AMRAP
4. 2 min foam roll

### "I Want to Go Heavy"
1. Full warmup with specific prep
2. Main lift from [[03 - Strength Blocks]] (S1-S6)
3. Skip conditioning or do 5 min easy cardio
4. Core stability work

### "I Want to Sweat"
1. Cardio warmup (Row/Bike 3-5 min)
2. Dynamic mobility circuit
3. Light strength (bodyweight or KB)
4. Long conditioning from [[04 - Conditioning Blocks]] (C1, C4, C8)

### "Active Recovery Day"
1. Light row/bike 10 min
2. Extended dynamic mobility
3. TGU practice 5 each side
4. Foam roll 10+ min
