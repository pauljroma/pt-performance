## Strength

### 1. EMOM 12 min
| Minute | Exercise | Reps | Notes |
|--------|----------|------|-------|
| 1 | Burpees | 10 | Full extension |
| 2 | V-Ups / Dead Bugs | 30" | Choose based on ability |
| 3 | Bike/Row | 30" | Moderate pace |
| 4 | Bicep Curls | 15 | Light-moderate weight |

**Format:** Complete work within minute, rest remainder

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** 8 burpees, 20" V-ups, 20" bike, 10 curls
> - **Intermediate:** As written
> - **Advanced:** 12 burpees, 45" V-ups, max cal bike, 20 curls heavier

| 2 | 8e | Moderate | 7 |
| 3 | 8e | Moderate | 7 |
| 4 | 8e | Moderate | 7 |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Handstand Walk practice between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options

> - **Intermediate:** Single-Leg Deadlift as written
> - **Advanced:** Deficit Single-Leg Deadlift or heavier load
