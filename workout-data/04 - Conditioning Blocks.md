# Conditioning Blocks

Select based on your phase and available time.

## Conditioning Format Guide

| Phase | Format | Duration | Intensity |
|-------|--------|----------|-----------|
| Foundation | Long AMRAP | 15-20 min | Moderate |
| Strength | EMOM / Short circuits | 8-12 min | High |
| Power | Intervals w/ rest | 10-15 min | Very High |
| Hypertrophy | Giant sets / Supersets | 15-20 min | Moderate-High |
| Recovery | Steady state | 20-30 min | Low |

---

# AMRAP Blocks (As Many Rounds As Possible)

## Block C1: Full Body AMRAP (15 min)
- 12 cal Row/Bike
- 12e Russian Twist
- 12 Burpees
- 12 Push Press
- 12 MB Slams

---

## Block C2: Lower Body AMRAP (15 min)
- 15 Goblet Squats
- 15 KB Swings
- 15e Lunges
- 15 Box Jumps/Step Ups

---

## Block C3: Upper Body AMRAP (12-15 min)
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 TRX Rows
- 10 KB Push Press
- 10e Renegade Rows

---

## Block C4: Chipper AMRAP (20 min)
- 500m Row or 1000m Bike
- 50 Sit Ups
- 75 Air Squats
- 25e Deadbugs
- 500m Row/1000m Bike
- 50 KB Swings
- 50e Mountain Climbers
- 25 Burpees

---

## Block C5: Ladder AMRAP (15 min)
Ascending ladder: 4, 8, 12, 16, 20...
- KB Swings
- Thrusters
- V Ups

---

# EMOM Blocks (Every Minute On the Minute)

## Block C6: 3-Station EMOM (9-12 min)
- Min 1: 10 cal Row/Bike
- Min 2: 12 BB Deadlifts
- Min 3: 10 Bar Over Burpees

---

## Block C7: 4-Station EMOM (12-16 min)
- Min 1: 12 Thrusters
- Min 2: 12 Deadlifts
- Min 3: 12 Burpees
- Min 4: 6e Rev. Lunge + Twist

---

## Block C8: 2-Station EMOM (12 min)
**Min 1:**
- KB Swing x 8
- Mountain Climbers x 15e

**Min 2:**
- TRX Row x 12
- Thrusters x 7-9

---

## Block C9: Core EMOM (9 min)
- Min 1: 15 V Ups
- Min 2: 20e Russian Twists
- Min 3: 30 sec Plank Hold

# Interval Blocks

## Block C14: Sprint Intervals
**5 Rounds:**
- 200m Row (or 400m Bike) - ALL OUT
- Rest 1:1 (match work time)

---

## Block C15: Tabata (4 min per exercise)
20 sec work / 10 sec rest x 8 rounds
Choose 2-3 exercises:
- KB Swings
- Burpees
- Mountain Climbers
- Air Squats

---

## Block C16: 30/30 Intervals (15 min)
30 sec work / 30 sec rest
Rotate through:
- Row/Bike
- KB Swings
- Burpees
- Slamball

---

# Pyramid Blocks

## Block C17: Pyramid AMRAP (20-25 min)

---

## Block C18: Descending Pyramid (12-15 min)
21-15-9:

# Buy-In / Cash-Out Blocks

## Block C19: Row Buy-In
**Buy-In:** Row 1500m or Bike 3000m

**3 Rounds:**
- 10 Devils Press
- 20 TRX Rows
- 30 Sit Ups

---

## Block C20: Cash-Out Finisher
After main workout:
- 500m Row
- Ladder Drills
- 1 min Wall Sit
- Jump Rope x 150
