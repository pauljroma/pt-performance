## Strength

### 1. Sumo Deadlift
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10 | Light | 5 |
| 2 | 8 | Moderate | 6 |
| 3 | 8 | Moderate | 7 |
| 4 | 6 | Heavy | 8 |

**Rest:** 90-120 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Pigeon & Child's Pose between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** Sumo Deadlift 4 x 10 (light weight, focus on form)
> - **Intermediate:** Sumo Deadlift as written
> - **Advanced:** Add deficit or increase load on final set

### 2. Reverse Snow Angel
| Set | Reps | Load | RPE |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 10-15 | Bodyweight | 6 |
| 2 | 10-15 | Bodyweight | 6 |
| 3 | 10-15 | Bodyweight | 7 |

**Rest:** 60 sec between sets
**Accessory:** Hollow Rock Hold 30-45 sec between sets

> [!tip] Scaling Options
> - **Beginner:** 3 x 8 with shorter ROM
> - **Intermediate:** Reverse Snow Angel as written
> - **Advanced:** Add light weight or increase tempo
