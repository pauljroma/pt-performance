# HIIT Cardio Session

## Cardio
- Light Jog - 3 min
- High Knees - 1 min
- Butt Kicks - 1 min

## Dynamic Stretch
- Arm Circles - 2x10
- Leg Swings - 2x10 each side
- Jumping Jacks - 2x20
- Mountain Climbers - 2x10

## Prehab
- Glute Bridges - 2x12
- Band Pull-Aparts - 2x15

## Functional
- Burpees - 4x10 @ RPE 9
- Box Jumps - 4x8 @ RPE 8
- Battle Ropes - 4x30 sec @ RPE 9
- Kettlebell Swings - 4x15 @ RPE 8
- Mountain Climbers - 4x20 @ RPE 8
- Jump Squats - 4x12 @ RPE 8
- Sprint Intervals - 6x30 sec @ RPE 9
- Rowing Sprints - 4x250m @ RPE 9

## Recovery
- Light Walk - 3 min
- Deep Breathing - 2 min
- Full Body Stretch - 5 min
