## 🎉 What's New

### 1. 🗓️ Patient Scheduling System

**Plan your workouts in advance!**

- **Calendar View:** See all your scheduled sessions in a weekly or monthly calendar
- **Smart Scheduling:** Pick dates and times that work for you
- **Reminders:** Get notified before your workouts
- **Flexible Management:** Easily reschedule or cancel sessions
- **Track Progress:** See upcoming, completed, and past due sessions

**How to use:**
1. Tap "Schedule" from your program view
2. Select a session from your program
3. Pick a date and time
4. Optionally add notes
5. Get reminded before your workout!

---

### 2. 📚 Workout Templates Library (Therapists)

**Save time creating programs!**

- **Reusable Templates:** Create workout templates you can assign to multiple patients
- **Smart Organization:** Browse by category (Strength, Rehab, Mobility, etc.) and difficulty level
- **Quick Assignment:** Convert templates to patient programs with one tap
- **Track Popularity:** See which templates are most used
- **Search & Filter:** Find the perfect template fast

**How to use:**
1. Create a great program for one patient
2. Save it as a template
3. Assign it to other similar patients
4. Customize as needed

---

### 3. 📊 Progress Charts & Analytics

**Visualize your improvements!**

- **Training Volume:** See total weight lifted over time
- **Strength Progression:** Track estimated 1RM improvements
- **Consistency Tracking:** Monitor workout completion rates
- **Streaks:** Track consecutive weeks of consistent training
- **Personal Records:** Celebrate your PRs!

**Charts Available:**
- Volume Chart (bar chart showing weekly volume)
- Consistency Chart (completion rate with goal line)
- Summary Cards (total volume, completion %, current streak)
- Time Period Selector (week, month, 3 months, 6 months, year, all time)

---

### 4. 🎥 Exercise Videos (Infrastructure Ready)

**Watch proper form demonstrations!**

- **Video Library:** Exercise videos with professional demonstrations
- **Form Cues:** Key coaching points overlaid on videos
- **Pause & Review:** Watch between sets to check your form
- **Offline Ready:** Download videos for offline viewing (coming soon)

**Note:** Content is being added progressively. Not all exercises have videos yet.

---

### 5. 🥗 Nutrition Tracking (Basic)

**Track your nutrition alongside training!**

- **Meal Logging:** Log meals with calories and macros
- **Nutrition Goals:** See daily targets set by your therapist
- **Progress Tracking:** Compare actual intake to goals
- **Meal Types:** Organized by breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks

**How to use:**
1. Tap "Nutrition" from the main menu
2. Log your meals throughout the day
3. Track calories, protein, carbs, and fats
4. See how you're doing against your goals

## 📱 How to Get Started

### For Patients:

1. **Schedule Your Workouts:**
   - Open your program
   - Tap "Schedule" on any session
   - Pick a date and time
   - Set reminders

2. **Track Your Progress:**
   - Tap "Progress" from the main menu
   - View volume and consistency charts
   - Select different time periods
   - Celebrate your improvements!

3. **Watch Exercise Videos:**
   - Tap any exercise with a video icon
   - Watch the demonstration
   - Review form cues
   - Watch between sets as needed

4. **Log Nutrition:**
   - Tap "Nutrition" from the main menu
   - Log each meal
   - Enter macros if known
   - Track progress to goals

### For Therapists:

1. **Create Templates:**
   - Build a successful program
   - Tap "Save as Template"
   - Categorize and add tags
   - Share with your practice or keep private

2. **Assign Templates:**
   - Open Templates library
   - Browse or search
   - Preview the template
   - Assign to patient with one tap
   - Customize as needed

3. **Set Nutrition Goals:**
   - Open patient profile
   - Set daily calorie and macro targets
   - Add notes about specific goals
   - Track patient progress

4. **Monitor Engagement:**
   - See which patients watch exercise videos
   - Track workout consistency
   - Review nutrition logging adherence
