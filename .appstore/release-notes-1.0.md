Welcome to Modus.

Version 1.0 introduces the core performance training system designed for athletes preparing for a specific competition or season.

Features available include:
- Structured training plans organized around a target event
- Strength, sprint, and conditioning workout tracking
- Performance metrics including lifts, performance, and outputs
- Daily workout guidance for focused training sessions
- Coming soon - wearable readiness integration

This is the first release of Modus and we will be adding additional performance analytics, training customization, and sport-specific programs in upcoming updates.
