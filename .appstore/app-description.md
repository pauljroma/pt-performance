# App Store Description — Modus

Modus is a performance training system designed for athletes preparing for a specific goal, season, or event. Instead of generic workouts, Modus builds a structured training plan that integrates strength, speed, mobility, conditioning, and recovery into one coordinated program.

Athletes train best when preparation is intentional. Modus organizes training around a target date—such as a season start, tryout, competition, or return-to-play timeline—and provides a progressive plan that balances workload, recovery, and measurable performance improvements.

The system combines proven training principles used by strength coaches, performance labs, and professional athletes:

• Structured strength programs based on progressive overload
• Sprint, jump, and power development for explosive performance
• Mobility and durability work to support long-term training
• Sport-specific conditioning and movement drills
• Recovery protocols to sustain high training output

Modus tracks what matters: sets, reps, load, sprint times, jump height, and key athletic outputs. Athletes can monitor progress over time and understand how their work translates into measurable performance gains.

Training sessions are organized into clear daily blocks so athletes can focus on execution rather than planning. Each workout provides guidance for the exact work to be completed that day.

For athletes who want deeper performance insight, Modus can integrate data from wearable devices to monitor readiness and recovery trends. This allows training intensity to be adjusted dynamically while still respecting the overall training plan.

Modus is designed for serious athletes and competitors preparing for:

• Baseball and throwing sports
• Field and court sports
• Speed and power development
• Off-season strength cycles
• Return-to-competition preparation

## Key Features
